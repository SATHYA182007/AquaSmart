# AquaSmart AI

AquaSmart AI is a smart agriculture SaaS platform designed to improve water management in farming through automation, real-time monitoring and intelligent decision support. The platform focuses on solving real agricultural challenges related to irrigation, water quality, fertigation and resource efficiency.

It is built as a professional agri-tech dashboard experience where farmers, agricultural managers and researchers can monitor field conditions, automate irrigation workflows, track water quality, manage fertigation schedules and view data-driven insights in one place.

---

## Problem Statement

Water is one of the most critical resources in agriculture, yet farmers continue to face major issues such as water scarcity, inefficient irrigation, poor water quality monitoring, fertilizer wastage and lack of automation.

AquaSmart AI addresses these problems by providing a digital platform that enables:

- Smart irrigation automation
- Water quality monitoring
- Precision fertigation management
- AI-based irrigation recommendations
- Farm analytics and decision support

---

## Project Objective

The main objective of AquaSmart AI is to build a smart water management platform that helps improve agricultural productivity while ensuring sustainable and efficient use of water and nutrients.

This project aims to:

- Reduce water wastage
- Improve irrigation efficiency
- Monitor irrigation water quality
- Optimize fertilizer usage through fertigation
- Provide a clean and professional SaaS-based agricultural dashboard

---

## Key Features

### 1. Dashboard
A complete overview of important agricultural data such as:

- Soil moisture
- Water quality status
- Fertigation activity
- Pump status
- Water usage
- Alerts and notifications
- AI recommendations

### 2. Smart Irrigation
Allows irrigation monitoring and control with features like:

- Zone-wise irrigation tracking
- Irrigation scheduling
- Manual and automated irrigation control
- Moisture threshold settings

### 3. Water Quality Monitoring
Tracks important irrigation water quality parameters such as:

- pH
- TDS
- EC
- Turbidity
- Water temperature

### 4. Precision Fertigation
Helps manage fertilizer delivery along with irrigation by monitoring:

- Crop type
- Fertilizer dosage
- NPK ratio
- Growth stage
- Fertigation schedules

### 5. AI Recommendations
Provides intelligent farming suggestions such as:

- When to irrigate
- When to delay irrigation
- Water quality warnings
- Fertigation suggestions

### 6. Farm Zones Management
Supports multiple fields or farm zones with separate tracking for:

- Crop assignments
- Moisture status
- Irrigation conditions
- Zone-level monitoring

### 7. Alerts and Notifications
Displays important updates and warning messages for:

- Low soil moisture
- Poor water quality
- Pump overload
- Rain forecast
- Fertigation issues

### 8. Analytics and Reports
Provides data-driven insights with:

- Water usage analytics
- Fertilizer usage tracking
- Irrigation history
- Water quality logs
- Operational reports
